Channel Chat UI Test

1. Navigate to Channel Chat

Go to: http://localhost:3001/dashboard?tab=channels

ℹ️ Make sure you're signed in first

2. Expected Channel Display

You should see the Exponent channel with:

  • ✅ Status: Ready
  • ✅ Video count: 3 videos
  • ✅ Green "Ready for chat" status

3. Click on the Channel

When you click on the Exponent channel, you'll be redirected to the chat interface.

The chat interface should have:

  • Main chat area on the right
  • Video references sidebar on the left (appears after AI mentions videos)

4. Test Questions

Try these questions to see video references appear:

1. "What interview strategies are discussed across the different videos?"
2. "Summarize the key points from each video in this channel"
3. "What companies are mentioned in the interview videos?"

ℹ️ The left sidebar will populate with referenced videos as the AI mentions them

5. Video References Sidebar

When videos are referenced, the sidebar shows:

  • Video thumbnail (if available)
  • Video title
  • Clickable timestamps that link to YouTube at the exact moment
  • "Watch video" link

✅ Each timestamp is clickable and opens YouTube at that exact time

6. Email Notification

The completion email should show:

  • ✅ "3 videos indexed" (not 0)
  • ✅ Channel title: Exponent
  • ✅ Link to start chatting
