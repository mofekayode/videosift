Testing Channel Chat Component

The ChannelChatInterface component has been updated with:

Key Features:

  • ✅ Video reference extraction from AI responses
  • ✅ Left sidebar showing referenced videos
  • ✅ Clickable timestamps linking to YouTube
  • ✅ Video thumbnails and titles

How it works:

  1. When the AI mentions a video like: "In the video 'Data Analyst Interview' at [2:45]..."
  2. The component extracts the video title and timestamp
  3. It matches the title to videos in the channel
  4. Shows the video in the left sidebar with clickable timestamps

Functions added:

  - extractVideoReferences(): Extracts video mentions from AI response
  - updateReferencedVideos(): Updates the sidebar state
  - convertTimestampToSeconds(): Converts timestamps for YouTube links
